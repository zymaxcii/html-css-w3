Dufu is the definition of precision engineering.

Since our formation, Dufu has been committed to excellence in precision engineering.

Dufu specializes in manufacturing precision machining parts and components. We have been supplying critical products and expertise needed to manufacture complex components and equipment used in industries such as HDD storage, medical, and others since 1987. Our solutions have been used for innovations around the world.

Ever think about what it takes to build servers which store your data and power your applications?

What does it take to make a car and build planes? We have world-class facilities to build precision engineered components that power technology, industries, communities, homes and people.

Dufu has full suite of design, prototyping, production and supply chain management capabilities, making us an ideal one-stop manufacturing facility for our customers. Not only do we have the experience and the expertise, we have the complete range of tools, equipment and machinery, to help transfer inventions into machines or products that satisfy demanding accuracy, repeatability and speed requirements.
