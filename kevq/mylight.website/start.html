Getting Started

So you decided you want to take part in this little series and want to make a Light Website of your own? That's great. This page will get you started.

This series works a bit like a wizard. Once you click on the Let's Go! button below, you will be taken to the first stage of the series. The site will be extremely basic and contain pretty much no formatting.

At the bottom of each step there's a link to the Next Step. This will take you to the next step in the series were you can add a little more to your site. Eventually you will get to the end of the series and will have a site that looks just like this. Good luck!

If you have any problems with this series, or find a bug, please contact me.
