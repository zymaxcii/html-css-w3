Responsiveness

What is responsiveness?

Put simply, responsiveness is when a website responds to certain conditions. For example, if you're on a smaller screen like a mobile phone, a responsive website will automatically change the layout of the page to suit the smalle screen.

That's what we just did here!

Try visiting this website on a mobile phone and see what happens to the menu. Pretty cool, ey?

If you don't have your mobile phone to hand, you could just shrink this window in your browser, then keep shrinking it to see what happens.
